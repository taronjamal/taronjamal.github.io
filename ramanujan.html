Srinivasa Ramanujan

Born in the winter of 1887, Srinivasa Ramanujan was a brilliant mathematician originating from Tamil Nadu, India. Despite lacking a formal education in pure mathematics, some consider him one of the most influential thinkers in the discipline. Blessed with the extraordinary ability to intuitively understand different areas of mathematics, he would go on to obtain great acclaim for his overall contribution(s). Ramanujan was a devout orthodox Hindu and strict vegetarian.

"Srinivasa Ramanujan was the strongest man in all of mathematics, probably in the entire history of science. He has been compared to a bursting supernova, illiminating the darkest, most profound corner of mathematics, before tragically struck down by tuberculosis at the age of 33.." (Michio Kaku, 1994)
